My passionate journey in pursuing education
2005-2016
Fine Art
There was not specific point I decided to learn Art in depth, There was plenty of opportunities to find what I like, and my favorite was drawing, coloring from kid among the any others.
2017-2020
Graphic Design / Motion Graphic
As I explored art, graphic design caught my eye. I started with Photoshop and Illustrator, then learned After Effects and Procreate to add interactivity.

I began at a community college to explore different courses before choosing a major. In my last semester, I took a UX Design class and knew it was exactly what I wanted to do.

Transferring to a UX Design program was tough since most undergrad majors were in general art, and close major, Human-Computer Interaction was mostly offered at the graduate level. So, I studied Fine Art, planning to pursue HCI later.

Although I didn’t expect to get degrees at the community college, but I obtained those by taking numerous design courses.
2022-Current
UX Design, Interaction Design, Software engineering
