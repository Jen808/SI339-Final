    Branding Design
    I came up with 12 different names and my finalist were Roomify and LivMate. To highlight the goal is not just for find home, find a place with roommate, I decided to go with LivMate
    The reason I choose to go with Top middle font is it has vertical component but also have square shape. In addiiton, mixed of diagonal line on 'e' makes 'LivMate' more inclusive for all different type of people
    Choose Green to give peaceful, refreshment. Among three color palettes, vivid group looks most engaging

    Hi-Fi Prototype
    Home
    The Home page in the LivMate app allows users to browse available housing and roommate listings. Users can toggle between ‚ÄúList‚Äù and ‚ÄúMap‚Äù views, apply filters for price, room type, and features, and use the search bar to find listings by city or address. Listings are displayed as scrollable cards with key details like price, location, and room type, along with save options. The bottom navigation bar provides easy access to Saved, Chat, and Profile pages.
    Listing Detail Page
    The Listing Detail page in the LivMate app provides key information about a housing option, including the number of rooms, bathrooms, and amenities like A/C, parking, and pet policies. Users can view room availability, pricing, and whether the room is shared or furnished.

    The resident's profile shows personal preferences like guest policies and noise tolerance. Users can save the listing, send a message, or view the location on a map.
    Map
    The Map View in the LivMate app lets users explore housing listings by location. Icons on the map represent listings, which users can tap to view details like price and room type. Users can switch between "List" and "Map" views and apply filters to refine the results. Clicking a listing shows a preview with key details.
    Chat
    The Chat feature in LivMate lets users communicate directly with homeowners or roommates, view ongoing conversations, and inquire about listings.

    In the Chat Detail view, users can easily send messages while seeing key listing info at the top. Clicking on the profile photo brings up the homeowner or roommate's profile details, and clicking on the background or room photo opens the full listing details.
    Profile
    The Profile page in LivMate allows users to manage their personal information and listing preferences. Users can edit contact details, control the visibility of their profile photo, sex, and age, and add housing listings under the "My Listing" section. The page also provides access to customize home features, room availability, and personal preferences, making it easy for users to tailor their profile and listings to attract potential roommates.
