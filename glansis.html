    • Problem Statement
    User Scenario #1
    A science teacher needs a lesson for their 8th grade students which is aligned to the next generation science standard on ecosystems.

    They would also like an age appropriate video to accompany their lesson
    Problem 1
    Hard to understand for people especially for 8th grade students due to professional words and complicated datas.

    GLANSIS needs more graphic elements to make a wide age range of users, what each word represents, what all figures represent, and what is expected to be based on the data.
    User Scenario #2
    4th grade student has been assigned to write a paper on a local invasive species (e.g. Zebra Mussels)

    Key Design Decision #1
    Easily expectable

    If users can't anticipate how something will work, they can easily become confused and find it complicated.
    Buttons
    By giving border and drop shadow, easy to recognize interactive elements
    Horizontal Slider
    Showing next page of horizontal slide, easy to understand it is shiftable. Continuous background keep united
    Habergur Menu Icon
    44px by 44px which satisfying WCAG guideline makes intuitive
    Scroll bar
    By cutting contents, user can think there is hidden contents without distracting the page by vulnerable contents
    Presentation Slide
    Page indicator at the bottom right with hover effect
    Key Design Decision #2
    Know where they are

    Users need to know their current location within the system. If they are about five steps away from the main page, they might lose track of where they are, making it difficult to navigate back to a specific page. By providing a persistent navigation trail, we can guide users and help them become more familiar and comfortable with the system.
    Page Name
    For Mobile, all pages title and upper catergory is display. For Desktop, Breadcrumbs are availble
    Emphasis page on menu
    For both Mobile and Desktop, tool/page where they exploring is selected
    Key Design Decision #3
    Make user to keep explore, keep in touch

    Make them to get to know more about GLANSIS, led them to be more interested in by marketing strategies, psychological methods.
    Illustrator for sub categories
    Neat research pages with visualized data
    Playable video
    Including video player within the page will not require extra step to play
    Social Media
    User can see recent updates and this can make user to follow account, increasing GLANSIS's social media engagement
