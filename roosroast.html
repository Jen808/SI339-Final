    Executive Summary
    RoosRoast Coffee, an iconic Ann Arbor coffee shop, aims to revamp its digital presence through a comprehensive redesign of its website, roosroast.com, to better reflect its quirky personality and enhance user engagement.

    Despite its success with two physical locations and a growing e-commerce platform, the current website falls short of capturing the in-store experience's quirkiness, community spirit, and spontaneous culture.
    Core Vision
    The redesign aims to enhance overall user experience by:
    • Emphasizing storytelling and community building to reflect the RoosRoast charm
    • Transform the current platform into an engaging digital experience that showcases RoosRoast's warm, communal atmosphere.

    Key Design Decision #2
    Making the add to cart button distinguished on the product page.
    Important buttons need visual prominence
    Making the add to cart button blend in with the background here can make it tougher to visually distinguish, where there should be unique styling (Baymard Reference #791). The one-time purchase button is most prominent currently.

    Instead, we‚Äôve added the filled in pop of color to the add to cart button to reinforce its highest importance on the page.
    Key Design Decision #3
    Including Save for Later, Your Likes, and subscription features into the cart experience
    Community & moments are
    at the core of RoosRoast
    Understanding users often use the shopping cart for comparison and storage (Baymard #620), we've enhanced its functionality to better serve this purpose.

    Features like "Save for Later," product descriptions, subscription options, and a "Your Likes" section have been added to the cart experience, alongside a comprehensive price breakdown to aid decision-making. The redesign provides users with the necessary tools to be informed and confident when buying from the RoosRoast site.
