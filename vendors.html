    Objectives
    This project facilitates interaction between street vendors and customers, addressing challenges exacerbated by COVID-19, which reduced street vendor availability and complicated research efforts.
    Problem Statement / Pain Points
    The number of street vendors has markedly decreased due to the impact of COVID-19.

    The pandemic has driven a significant shift towards online shopping and delivery services, leading to a decline in the presence of street vendors at their usual times and locations.

    By enabling vendors to notify their regular customers of their operating hours and locations, there is potential to enhance communication and better meet the needs of both parties.
